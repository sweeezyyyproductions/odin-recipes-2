Chicken Alfredo

Description

This chicken Alfredo recipe is a creamy and flavorful pasta dish that can be prepared quickly and easily for a satisfying dinner. It features tender chicken breast strips coated in a rich Alfredo sauce made with heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and garlic. The dish is served over cooked fettuccine pasta and garnished with fresh parsley for added freshness.

For a quick and simple weeknight dinner, this recipe fits the bill perfectly. The steps are straightforward, and the ingredients are commonly found in most kitchens. The chicken is cooked in a skillet, while the Alfredo sauce is prepared in the same pan, infusing it with the delicious flavors from the chicken. Tossing the cooked pasta in the sauce completes the dish, which can be ready in under 30 minutes.

On the other hand, this chicken Alfredo can also be elevated to a main dish for a larger dinner party. To make it suitable for a crowd, you can easily scale up the recipe by doubling or tripling the ingredients. The dish can be prepared in advance, and you can keep the cooked pasta separate from the sauce until ready to serve. When your guests arrive, simply reheat the sauce, combine it with the cooked pasta, and serve it with beautifully arranged chicken slices on top. The creamy and indulgent flavors of the Alfredo sauce make it an appealing option for guests, and it pairs well with a variety of side dishes like garlic bread, salad, or roasted vegetables.

Overall, this chicken Alfredo recipe strikes a balance between simplicity and elegance. Whether you're looking for a quick and satisfying dinner or a main dish to impress your guests at a dinner party, this recipe is versatile enough to meet both needs.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • Sizes may vary depending on feeding sizes
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through and no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside to rest.
  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant. Reduce the heat to low.
  4. Add the heavy cream, butter, and grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet. Stir continuously until the sauce is smooth and creamy, and the cheese is fully melted. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Slice the cooked chicken into thin strips.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet with the Alfredo sauce and toss until the pasta is well coated.
  7. Divide the pasta onto serving plates and top with the sliced chicken. Garnish with fresh parsley.
  8. Serve the chicken Alfredo immediately and enjoy!
